# Positioning and Messaging

Last updated: February 27, 2026

## Positioning statement

Writing is how people clarify thinking. The problem is not "people cannot type words."  
The problem is that blank pages create friction, and public permanence creates self-censorship.

This product is a private writing environment where users can start from messy thoughts and quickly shape clear posts in their own voice, then keep published posts contextually current over time.

## What we are not

- Not a generic chatbot.
- Not a pure voice transcription app.
- Not an "AI writer that replaces your thinking."

## What we are

- An idea-to-draft partner.
- A private reflection and publishing studio.
- A living publication system with post-publish freshness intelligence.

## Messaging pillars

## 1) Start from thoughts, not blank pages

- Users can paste rough notes, fragments, or voice transcriptions.
- The system creates a strong first draft that still feels like the user wrote it.
- Users remain in editorial control.

## 2) Sound like yourself, not AI slop

- Personal style profile (tone, cadence, vocabulary, paragraph rhythm).
- "Anti-slop" pass removes generic patterns and repetitive punctuation behavior.
- Always offer multiple rewrite choices instead of one imposed rewrite.

## 3) Publish once, stay current

- Agents monitor post-publish factual drift.
- The app surfaces subtle context updates and suggested patch notes.
- No silent rewrites of published content.

## Brand language guidelines

Use:

- "clarify your thinking"
- "start with messy ideas"
- "private by default"
- "refine in your voice"
- "context updates"

Avoid:

- "just dictate and we do everything"
- "fully automated writing"
- "AI writes for you"
