Stop Shopping for Productivity Systems. Start Using One.

I've tried them all — Notion, Roam, Obsidian, Logseq, plain text files. I've built elaborate second brains and abandoned them within a week. And somewhere between my third "fresh start" and my fourth app migration, I realized the obvious thing nobody wants to hear:

The app doesn't matter. The habit does.

A friend of mine uses index cards. Paper ones, from the dollar store. His output is three times mine on a bad week. He doesn't think about his system — he just uses it. That's the entire lesson, and no productivity app can teach it for you.
